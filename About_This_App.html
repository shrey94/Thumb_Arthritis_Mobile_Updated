Welcome!






This program was created with the Curtis National Hand Center at MedStar Union Memorial Hospital to help people with thumb arthritis decrease their pain and increase their strength and function.
Perform these exercises as pain allows once a day for several months – just like working out you shouldn’t expect results in just a few days or weeks! We typically recommend a 2-to-4-month initial trial.
For more information on thumb arthritis, you can view this video one of our former fellows made. Another good independent source, including a decision-making tool, is www.decisionaid.info and the topic “Thumb arthrosis”.
